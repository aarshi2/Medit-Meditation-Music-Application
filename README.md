# Medit-Meditation-Music-Application
Medit is a Flutter-based meditation music application designed to help users relax, reduce stress, and improve mindfulness through calming music and nature sounds. The app provides a simple and user-friendly interface where users can explore different categories of meditation music, create a personalized list of favorite tracks, and enjoy uninterrupted audio playback.

The application focuses on promoting mental well-being by offering peaceful music suitable for meditation, yoga, sleep, study, and relaxation. With a clean design and smooth navigation, Medit creates a calming digital experience that encourages users to maintain a healthy and mindful lifestyle.
